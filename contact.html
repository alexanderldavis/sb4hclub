How to Reach Us

The best way to contact us is through Facebook, by email (sb4hclub@gmail.com), or by calling 1-(650)-200-0448. The San Bruno 4-H Club meets the second Wednesday of each month at the San Bruno 4-H Clubhouse. Meetings begin at 7PM and run approximately 1 hour.

You are welcome to attend any general meeting. There is a raffle and valuable information about 4-H. You may also attend one of our scheduled Open Houses.

San Mateo County

Learn more about the San Mateo County 4-H program by visiting their website. Or, contact us above to get more information about how to join. We usually process requests in under 24 hours.
