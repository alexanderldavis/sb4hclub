How to Enroll

The best way to join is through Facebook, Google Plus, by email (sb4hclub@gmail.com), or by calling 1-(650)-200-0448.

Want to join today? Follow these directions to get registered with 4-H! Contact us with the above information to learn how to join. We usually process requests in under 24 hours.

Informational Meetings

The San Bruno 4-H Club meets the second Wednesday of each month at the San Bruno 4-H Clubhouse. Meetings begin at 7:00PM and run approximately 1 hour. You are welcome to attend any general meeting. There is a raffle and valuable information about 4-H. You may also attend one of our scheduled Open Houses.
